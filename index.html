About the Service

Need a legal certification of documents? This means you need a certified true copy of an original document (such as passports, proof of address, or any other document) to be able to present to a third party without having to send the original document itself!

Note that a certification does not certify that the primary document is genuine, only that it is a true copy of the primary document.

All you need to do is:

Book an appointment

Book an appointment here at a convenient time: Book Here or request a time via email at docucertmalta@gmail.com .

Prepare all documents

Please prepare all original documents and copies of the same documents which require the certification. You must bring both the original documents and the copies to be certified with you.

Come to your appointment

Make your way to your appointment in Birkirkara – see location here:

Certification

Your documents will be certified within minutes!!

Make payment
